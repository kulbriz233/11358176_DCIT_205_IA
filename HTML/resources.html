Computer Labs

These labs are equipped with state-of-the-art hardware and software necessary for programming, testing, and simulations. Library Resources

The department's library offers an extensive collection of resources including books, journals, research papers, and e-books covering diverse topics within computer science. Additionally, access to online databases and subscriptions to platforms like IEEE Xplore and ACM Digital Library complement the physical collection, ensuring students have a wealth of information at their disposal.

Online Learning Platforms

These platforms, ranging from Coursera and edX to Khan Academy and Codecademy, provide an array of courses, tutorials, and learning materials. They facilitate self-paced learning and exploration of specialized areas beyond the regular curriculum, allowing students to delve deeper into their interests.
