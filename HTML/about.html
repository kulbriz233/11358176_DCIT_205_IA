  • Welcome Once More

    This is the Information Technology age and at its core is Computer Science. The study of computer science is therefore critical if society is to take full advantage of the benefits Information Technology has to offer and make rapid economic advances. Computer science involves the study and development of applications that allow for our everyday use of computers, the software systems that support these applications, and the means and methods used to produce both applications and systems that behave reliably, correctly and ethically. At the heart of Computer Science lies the skill of computer programming and employers who hire Computer Scientists expect adept programmers with knowledge of current techniques and tools and the ability to apply sound engineering principles to the development, construction and maintenance of computer programs. The Department offers two undergraduate degree programmes, Bachelor of Science in Computer Science Single-Major (3:2:1:1) and Major-Minor (3:2:2:1) as well as Bachelor of Science in Information Technology.

Distance Learning

Distance study: Learning from anywhere using online tools and resources, offering flexibility in education.

  • Learning Made Simple For All

    So basically Distance learning, often referred to as online or remote education, has transformed the landscape of learning by transcending physical boundaries. It empowers individuals to pursue education from anywhere, offering flexibility and accessibility. Through digital platforms and tools, distance learning facilitates interactive experiences, enabling students to engage with diverse materials and collaborate with peers and educators globally. While it requires self-discipline and motivation, it opens doors to lifelong learning opportunities, catering to diverse schedules and circumstances. Distance learning continues to evolve, reshaping education by providing an inclusive and adaptable framework for acquiring knowledge and skills in the modern era.Distance Learning Experience a UG-CDCD classroom from wherever you are. Take advantage of real-time lectures and personal interaction with professors through a suite of online delivery options, customizing your degree to fit your schedule. Our online classroom maintains the same level of academic rigor, while providing students with more ways of learning.

Building Our Future

To make it simple computer science is our leading world weapon to make and destroy anything anytime, we better build it for a better future

  • Our Future Is A Better Tomorrow

    "Building Our Future" encapsulates the essence of computer science in today's world. This field serves as the cornerstone of innovation, shaping the very foundation upon which our future is constructed. Through the relentless pursuit of knowledge and technological advancements, computer science pioneers solutions that revolutionize industries, redefine possibilities, and propel us into uncharted territories.
    The convergence of computer science with cutting-edge technologies like artificial intelligence, machine learning, and quantum computing sets the stage for transformative breakthroughs. It empowers us to envision and create a future where intelligent systems enhance efficiency, where data-driven insights revolutionize decision-making, and where connectivity bridges gaps across borders and disciplines.
    As we navigate this digital age, the field of computer science stands as the architect of tomorrow, offering solutions to complex challenges, from cybersecurity to healthcare, from environmental sustainability to space exploration. Its practitioners, armed with curiosity, creativity, and technical prowess, are the builders of our future, constructing a world that is more connected, innovative, and adaptable than ever before.
